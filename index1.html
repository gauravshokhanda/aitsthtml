Appointment Policy

Our purpose is to provide high quality tax services in a timely manner. To achieve this, an appointment policy has been implemented to enhance the available appointments for our clients. The policy will monitor and manage appointments for no-shows, cancellations, and late arrivals.

Please consider appointment times are limited. We recognize there may be unexpected events occurring. If your agenda changes and you need to cancel or reschedule your appointment, we respectfully request at least a 24 hour notice for the tax preparer, our staff, and our clients.

Cancellation or reschedule notice with at least 24 hours prior to the scheduled appointment will be processed without a penalty.

“No Show” clients who fail to arrive for the scheduled appointment will incur a fee of $50.

“Same Day Cancellation” clients who cancel or reschedule appointment with less than 24 hours notice prior the scheduled appointment will incur a fee of $50.

“Late Arrival” clients who arrive at the office 15 minutes after the expected arrival time for the scheduled appointment will still be seen but the return may be converted to a drop off.

Reschedule your appointment if you are feeling unwell. Do your part to slow the spread of any illness for others.
